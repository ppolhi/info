Yin Yoga

  • Focus: Deep stretching and relaxation.
  • Description: A slow, meditative practice where poses are held for several minutes to stretch deep connective tissues and joints.
  • Benefits: Improves flexibility, reduces stress, and promotes mindfulness.

7. Restorative Yoga

  • Focus: Deep relaxation and healing.
  • Description: Involves restful poses held for extended periods, often supported by props, to release tension and restore energy.
  • Benefits: Reduces stress, calms the nervous system, and improves mental clarity.

8. Kundalini Yoga

  • Focus: Awakening spiritual energy.
  • Description: Combines postures, breathing techniques, chanting, and meditation to awaken kundalini energy at the base of the spine.
  • Benefits: Enhances spiritual awareness, boosts energy, and balances emotions.

9. Power Yoga

  • Focus: Fitness and strength.
  • Description: A vigorous style of Vinyasa yoga that emphasizes strength-building poses and faster sequences.
  • Benefits: Builds muscle, burns calories, and increases stamina.

10. Anusara Yoga

  • Focus: Heart-opening poses and positivity.
  • Description: A modern, alignment-focused style emphasizing grace, heart-centered themes, and mindfulness.
  • Benefits: Improves alignment, flexibility, and emotional balance.

11. Prenatal Yoga

  • Focus: Safe yoga for pregnant women.
  • Description: Tailored to the needs of pregnant individuals, focusing on gentle stretches, breathing, and relaxation.
  • Benefits: Reduces pregnancy-related discomfort, improves flexibility, and prepares for childbirth.

12. Chair Yoga

  • Focus: Accessibility for all ages and abilities.
  • Description: Adapted poses performed sitting in or using a chair for support, making yoga accessible for those with mobility limitations.
  • Benefits: Improves flexibility, balance, and circulation.
